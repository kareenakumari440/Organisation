About Me

Anganwadi is a rural child care center in India established under the Integrated Child Development Services (ICDS) program by the Government of India. Introduced in 1975, Anganwadi centers aim to combat child hunger and malnutrition by providing basic health care services to children under the age of six and their mothers. These services include supplementary nutrition, non-formal pre-school education, health check-ups, immunization, and referral services. Anganwadi workers and helpers play a crucial role in implementing these services at the grassroots level. Child Education

Child education is crucial for a child's development, preparing them for the future and empowering them to become successful, contributing members of society. It equips them with knowledge, skills, and values essential for leading fulfilling lives, and also allows them to make informed choices, exercise their rights, and participate in their communities. Education helps children develop mentally and emotionally, fostering curiosity and a lifelong love of learning

Women Empowerment

Women's empowerment encompasses granting women the autonomy to make their own decisions, pursue their aspirations, and participate equally in all aspects of life. This involves dismantling societal structures that perpetuate gender inequality and providing women with access to education, economic opportunities, and healthcare. Ultimately, women's empowerment aims to create a society where women and girls can thrive, contribute fully to their communities, and live with dignity and respect. .

Health Care

Healthcare encompasses the services and resources that maintain and improve a person's physical, mental, and social well-being. It aims to prevent, diagnose, and treat diseases, injuries, and other health issues. This care is provided by various healthcare professionals, including doctors, nurses, and allied health fields. The fundamental goal of healthcare is to enhance quality of life by promoting health.
